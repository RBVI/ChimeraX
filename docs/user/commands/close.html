Command: close

Usage:
close  model-spec

The command close removes the specified model(s) from ChimeraX, including any submodels at lower levels of the hierarchy. For example, close #3 will close any models #3.N, #3.N.N, etc. Submodels may arise when molecular surfaces are generated or an ensemble (such as from NMR) is opened. As per the usual convention, a blank specification indicates “all.” See also: open, hide, delete, quit

Examples:

close
close #1
close #2,4-6
close #3.2
close #3.2-10
